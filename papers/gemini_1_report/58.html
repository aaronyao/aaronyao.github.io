Multi-step reasoning and mathematics Prompt In the year 2018 factory A produced goods worth 500 million forints, while factory B produced goods worth 400 million forints. According to their long-term plans, factory A will increase production by 5% each year, while factory B will increase production by 6% per year. The above plans were published on an economy website. A debate started in the comment section of the article: one user claimed that the diﬀerence between the volumes of production between the two factories would get smaller and smaller in the next couple of years. Calculate the missing values of the table below and show that the above claim is not true.
